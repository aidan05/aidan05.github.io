Why Obi-Wan is a True Jedi

After fighting General Grievous, Obi-Wan would find out the clones had been ordered to destroy all the Jedi. He made his escape from the army that was once on his side, afterwards he would find out that the Jedi he had been training since he was only 9 years old, Anakin Skywalker, had betrayed the Jedi. It was up to Kenobi to either turn him back to the light, or stop him from causing anymore destruction. Anakin's wife Padme had twins shortly after this. Kenobi and Master Yoda decided to split the twins so Anakin could never find them. After deciding the planets the twins would be hidden on, both Kenobi and Yoda went into hiding. Many years later they would both train Luke Skywalker so he could destroy The Empire which was created by Anakin and his Sith Master Darth Sidious. Anakin would be named Darth Vader and eventually would be defeated by Luke.
